Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Introduction

MediSync is a centralized pharmacy inventory management platform developed and operated by TATA Consultancy Services Limited ("TCS", "we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the MediSync platform.

By accessing or using MediSync, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please discontinue use of the platform immediately.

Information We Collect

User Account Data

When you register for and use MediSync, we collect information necessary to create and manage your account, including:

  • Full name and employee identification
  • Email address and phone number
  • Role designation (pharmacist, vendor, administrator, etc.)
  • Organization or facility affiliation
  • Authentication credentials (encrypted passwords, session tokens)

Inventory and Operational Data

MediSync processes sensitive operational data essential to pharmacy management:

  • Medicine inventory records (stock levels, batch numbers, expiration dates)
  • Dispensing transactions and prescription fulfillment logs
  • Store Request for Supply (RFS) submissions and fulfillment records
  • Vendor bid submissions, pricing proposals, and contract terms
  • Expiry alerts and stock movement history
  • Quality assurance and compliance documentation

Technical and Usage Data

To ensure platform security, reliability, and performance optimization, we automatically collect:

  • IP addresses and device identifiers
  • Browser type, version, and operating system
  • Login timestamps and session duration
  • Pages accessed and actions performed within the platform
  • Error logs and diagnostic information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for legitimate business purposes related to pharmacy inventory management:

  • Account Management: Creating, maintaining, and authenticating user accounts with role-based access control
  • Operational Functionality: Enabling inventory tracking, dispensing workflows, RFS processing, and vendor bid evaluation
  • Compliance and Auditing: Maintaining transaction records for regulatory compliance and internal audits
  • Platform Security: Detecting and preventing unauthorized access, fraud, and system abuse
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance platform usability and performance
  • Communication: Sending system notifications, alerts, and critical operational updates

Vendor Bid Confidentiality

We recognize that vendor bid submissions contain commercially sensitive information. MediSync implements strict controls to protect vendor confidentiality:

  • Bid data is accessible only to authorized personnel directly involved in procurement evaluation
  • Vendor pricing proposals are not disclosed to competing vendors or unauthorized third parties
  • All bid-related communications are encrypted and access-logged
  • Vendor submission history is retained for contract management and compliance purposes only

Store RFS Data Protection

Request for Supply (RFS) submissions from pharmacy stores contain operational demand forecasts and inventory needs. We protect this data by:

  • Restricting access to authorized procurement and inventory management personnel
  • Encrypting RFS data during transmission and storage
  • Maintaining audit trails of all RFS access and modifications
  • Preventing unauthorized disclosure of store-specific inventory patterns

Data Sharing and Disclosure

MediSync does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or operational data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Within Your Organization: Data may be shared with authorized personnel within your pharmacy network or vendor organization as necessary for operational purposes
  • Service Providers: We may engage trusted third-party service providers (hosting, analytics, support) who are contractually obligated to protect your data
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred to the successor entity

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission (TLS/SSL)
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for privileged accounts
  • Role-based access controls and principle of least privilege
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems
  • Secure backup and disaster recovery procedures

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law:

  • Active user accounts: Retained for the duration of active employment or vendor relationship
  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years to comply with pharmaceutical regulatory requirements
  • Audit logs: Retained for 3 years for security and compliance purposes
  • Inactive accounts: Archived after 12 months of inactivity and purged after 3 years

Subject to applicable laws and organizational policies, you may have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information stored in MediSync
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your account (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact your organization's system administrator or reach out through the contact information provided below.
