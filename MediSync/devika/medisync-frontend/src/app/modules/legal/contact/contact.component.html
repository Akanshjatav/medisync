Getting in Touch

Whether you need technical assistance, have questions about using MediSync, or want to report an issue, our support team is ready to assist you. Please use the appropriate contact method based on the nature of your inquiry.

Technical Support

For issues related to platform functionality, account access, or technical errors:

MediSync Technical Support

  • Email: medisync-support@tcs.com
  • Support Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM IST
  • Response Time: Within 24 business hours

For urgent system outages or critical issues affecting patient care, please mark your email as "URGENT" in the subject line.

Account & Access Issues

For help with login problems, password resets, role assignments, or user account management:

Account Support

  • Email: medisync-accounts@tcs.com
  • First Step: Contact your organization's system administrator
  • Escalation: If your administrator is unable to resolve the issue, reach out to our account support team

Please include your full name, organization name, and a detailed description of the access issue in your request.

Before You Contact Us

To help us assist you more efficiently, please have the following information ready when reaching out:

  • Your Details: Full name, email address, organization name, and user role
  • Issue Description: Clear description of the problem or question
  • Screenshots: If applicable, include screenshots showing the issue
  • Reference Numbers: Any relevant batch numbers, RFS IDs, bid IDs, or error codes
  • Browser & Device: Information about your browser, operating system, and device type
  • Urgency Level: Indicate whether the issue is critical, urgent, or routine

Frequently Asked Questions

Many common questions can be answered through our knowledge base and user documentation. Before contacting support, you may find it helpful to:

  • Check the in-platform help documentation (click the "?" icon in the navigation bar)
  • Review the user guides available in your account dashboard
  • Consult with your organization's system administrator
  • Search for error codes or messages in our online knowledge base

Priority Level Description Response Time

Emergency Support

For critical system outages or security incidents outside of business hours that pose an immediate risk to patient safety or pharmacy operations, please use our emergency escalation procedures. Contact your organization's designated emergency coordinator, who has access to our 24/7 emergency support channel.

Note: Emergency support is reserved for critical incidents only. Non-urgent matters should be addressed through standard support channels during business hours.
