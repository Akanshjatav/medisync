Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about MediSync implementation and features.

How quickly can we get started with MediSync?

Answer: Implementation typically takes 1 to 2 weeks for multi branch pharmacies. Our team handles data migration, staff training, and system integration to ensure a smooth transition.

Does MediSync support regulatory compliance for controlled substances?

Answer: Yes, MediSync is fully equipped to handle Schedule H, Schedule X, and other regulated medicines with complete audit trails, license tracking, and automated compliance reporting.

Can our vendors interact directly with the system?

Answer: Absolutely. Our vendor portal enables suppliers to register, submit compliance documents, respond to tenders, update delivery status, and raise support tickets directly within the platform.

Is the system accessible on mobile devices?

Answer: Yes, MediSync offers responsive web access and dedicated mobile applications for pharmacists and managers to update inventory, check stock levels, and approve orders on the go.

What kind of reporting and analytics does MediSync provide?

Answer: MediSync includes comprehensive dashboards with real time analytics, inventory turnover reports, sales trends, expiry forecasts, vendor performance metrics, and customizable reports for audits and management review.
