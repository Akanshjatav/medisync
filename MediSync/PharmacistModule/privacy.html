Privacy Policy Updated: 31 Dec 2025

Who we are: MediSync (“we”, “us”) provides a dispensing and billing application for healthcare providers. We act as a Data Fiduciary (controller) when determining how personal data is processed.

1) Data We Collect

  • Account data: Name, contact details, branch, role, login logs.
  • Dispensing & billing: Patient/prescription identifiers, items dispensed, FEFO choices, manual override logs, invoices, payments.
  • Device & usage: App telemetry, crash logs, IP address, browser info.
  • Cookies: Essential session cookies; analytics only with consent.

2) Why We Process Data

We process data based on: (a) consent, (b) contractual necessity, (c) legitimate uses (security, fraud prevention), and (d) legal obligations (tax, compliance).

3) Children’s Data

We do not knowingly onboard minors. If required, we will obtain verifiable parental consent and avoid tracking or targeted ads for children.

4) How We Use Data

  • Provide dispensing and billing services.
  • Maintain audit trails and ensure FEFO compliance.
  • Improve security and prevent fraud.
  • Support and notify about recalls or safety issues.

5) Sharing

We share data only as needed with healthcare providers, payment processors, hosting services, and regulators—under confidentiality and security safeguards.

6) Security

We use encryption, role-based access, audit logs, and breach notifications as required by law.

7) Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for stated purposes or legal requirements, then securely delete it.

8) International Transfers

Data may be stored outside India with contractual safeguards. Transfers follow DPDP and, if applicable, GDPR rules.

9) Your Rights

  • Access, correction, erasure, consent withdrawal, grievance redressal.
  • If GDPR applies: portability, restriction, objection, and limits on automated decisions.

10) Contact

Mail to privacy@medisync.example If unresolved, you may contact the Data Protection Board of India or your local authority.

11) Changes

We will update this policy and notify you of significant changes.
