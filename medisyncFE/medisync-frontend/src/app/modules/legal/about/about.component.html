About MediSync

Transforming pharmacy inventory management through innovation

Our Mission

MediSync is a centralized pharmacy inventory management platform designed to streamline operations, enhance supply chain efficiency, and support healthcare professionals in delivering quality patient care. Built by TATA Consultancy Services Limited (TCS), MediSync bridges the gap between pharmacies, vendors, and healthcare facilities through intelligent automation and data-driven insights.

What We Do

MediSync provides a comprehensive suite of tools for managing pharmaceutical inventory across multiple locations, facilitating seamless communication between pharmacies and vendors, and ensuring regulatory compliance throughout the supply chain.

Core Capabilities

Centralized Inventory Management

Real-time tracking of pharmaceutical stock across multiple pharmacy locations, enabling efficient allocation and reducing waste from expired medications.

Dispensing & Transaction Tracking

Complete audit trail of medication dispensing activities, ensuring accountability and supporting regulatory compliance requirements.

Expiry Alert System

Proactive notifications for medications approaching expiration dates, helping pharmacies minimize financial losses and maintain quality standards.

Request for Supply (RFS) Management

Streamlined process for pharmacy stores to submit supply requests, track fulfillment status, and maintain optimal inventory levels.

Vendor Bid Platform

Secure system for pharmaceutical vendors to submit competitive bids, respond to RFS requests, and manage order fulfillment.

Role-Based Access Control

Granular permissions management ensuring that pharmacists, vendors, administrators, and other users access only the information relevant to their roles.

What We Don't Do

It's important to understand the scope and limitations of MediSync:

  • Not a Clinical Tool: MediSync is an inventory management platform, not a clinical decision support system. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.
  • Not Patient-Facing: The platform is designed exclusively for B2B use by healthcare professionals and vendors, not for direct patient access or consumer sales.
  • Not a Prescription System: MediSync tracks dispensing activities but does not generate or validate prescriptions. Clinical decisions remain the sole responsibility of licensed healthcare professionals.
  • Not a Quality Certifier: While we facilitate vendor-pharmacy transactions, MediSync does not verify product quality, authenticity, or regulatory compliance. These remain the responsibility of vendors and pharmacy quality assurance teams.

  • Mobile-Responsive Design: Full functionality on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Compliance & Standards

MediSync is designed to support compliance with pharmaceutical regulations and healthcare industry standards:

  • Audit trail maintenance for regulatory inspections
  • Batch number and expiry date tracking for product recalls
  • User authentication and access logging for data security compliance
  • Data retention policies aligned with pharmaceutical record-keeping requirements
  • Support for Good Distribution Practices (GDP) in pharmaceutical supply chains

Note: While MediSync facilitates compliance, ultimate responsibility for regulatory adherence rests with the pharmacies and vendors using the platform.
